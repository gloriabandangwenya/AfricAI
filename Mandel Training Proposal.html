Our Approach

Not generic training.
Role-specific transformation.

The difference between a training programme that gets a 10/10 rating and one that gets forgotten is specificity. Generic Copilot training teaches features. Role-specific Copilot training changes how a department works. Every session is built around the real daily tasks, workflows, and challenges of a specific professional role.

01 · Discovery First
We learn your team before we train them
A pre-training survey maps current usage, confidence levels, and specific workflow bottlenecks. A short briefing with the department head aligns on priorities and success criteria. By the time training begins, every example and use case is built around Delta's actual environment.
02 · Role-Specific Delivery
Built for your role, not for everyone
An HR professional and a Finance analyst have completely different Copilot needs, so each session is designed for one department's specific workflows: the tasks they do every day, the documents they create, the data they analyse, the communications they send.
03 · Reinforcement and Support
We stay after the training ends
Most training programmes deliver and disappear. We include post-training check-ins to address what comes up in real usage, reinforce new habits, and ensure adoption actually sticks beyond the training day itself.
04 · Measurable Outcomes
You see what changed
Every engagement includes an adoption progress report covering confidence shift before and after, usage patterns, key wins, and a clear recommendation for the next phase. You can demonstrate ROI to leadership and boards.

AI Governance

Protecting Delta's data
as Copilot scales

Copilot has access to your entire Microsoft 365 environment: emails, SharePoint, Teams, financial documents, HR records. Without governance, the risk of unintended data exposure, compliance issues, and misuse grows with every user who adopts it. Governance is not a compliance exercise. It is protection for your investment and your organisation.

📋
Acceptable Use Policy
A clear, practical policy defining what Copilot can and cannot be used for, tailored to Delta's specific risk profile and regulatory environment.
🔐
Data Access Framework
Guidance on what information Copilot can access, what should be restricted, and how to configure permissions to prevent unintended data exposure.
🎓
Staff Awareness Training
Every team member trained to understand AI boundaries: what to share, what not to share, and how to use Copilot responsibly with sensitive information.
📊
Incident Response Process
A clear protocol for identifying, reporting, and responding to any AI-related data incidents, ensuring Delta can act quickly if something goes wrong.
Delivery Process

The 30-day engagement model

Every training engagement follows a structured four-phase process designed to maximise adoption and ensure measurable results, not just a training day that gets forgotten by Friday.

Week 01
Discovery and Preparation
Pre-training survey sent to all participants covering current Copilot usage, confidence levels, daily workflow bottlenecks, and what they wish Copilot could do. A 20-minute briefing with the department head aligns on priorities and success criteria. All training content is built from these responses.
Week 02
Bespoke Training Delivery
A full-day or half-day session delivered in-person at Delta's premises or virtually. Every example, use case, and exercise is built around that department's actual workflows and documents. Participants leave with practical skills they can apply immediately, not a theoretical overview of features.
Week 03
First Reinforcement Check-in
A 60-minute group session addressing what came up in real usage during the first week back. Questions answered, workflows refined, habits reinforced. Most training programmes fail at this point as they disappear at this critical moment. We stay in.
Week 04
Second Check-in and Progress Report
Final reinforcement session followed by a comprehensive adoption progress report covering before and after confidence comparison, usage patterns observed, key wins documented, and a clear recommendation for the next phase. Mandel and Delta receive a full report they can present to leadership.
